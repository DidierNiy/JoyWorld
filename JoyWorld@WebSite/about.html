About Us

Empowering the Community for a Brighter Future

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
Joy World Mission organization is located in Kenya's Busia County, Madibo region of Bukhayo East Nambale. It was founded with the goal of improving the lives of the residents of the community. Among many other areas of impact in the community, Joy world Mission actively fundraises to support orphans, construct churches, provide free education to the underprivileged, facilitate the sinking of water wells, and distribute Bibles
Our mission is to:
a) inspire and support young people by providing necessary resources, skills, and opportunities for success.
b) Provide a livelihood for orphans through an orphanage facility.
c) Educate poor children in the community.
d) Provide support for church construction and Bible donations.
e) Providing clean water by sinking wells.
f) Provide food relief and donations to the poor elderly members in the society.
We believe that every young person has the potential to achieve greatness, and we are committed to helping them realize their dreams through faith-based teachings, mentorship, education, and community engagement.
We envision a world where all young people especially from poor backgrounds have the confidence and tools to lead successful, fulfilling lives. By fostering a sense of belonging and empowerment, we aim to cultivate a generation of resilient, compassionate, and proactive leaders.
